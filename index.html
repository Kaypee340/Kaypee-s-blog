Kobbie Mainoo starts for the England National team

  • The 18 years old mid-fielder made his first England debut on saturday against Brazil, coming on as a substitute after 75 minutes at Wembley stadium. Man united wonder kid, Kobbie mainoo starts for England in their last friendly match against Belgium , and wins the player of the match award. The 18 years old English mid-fielder has shown that he has potentials by winning the player of the match award in his first ever start for the England national senior team.

    Kobbie Mainoo has become the third youngest united player to start an england game at the age of 18 years and 342 days, behind Duncan Edwards [18 years and 183 days], and Marcus Rashford [18 years and 209 days].

    "Everything that we've seen with his club, he reproduced in an England shirt which isn't always the case". "GARETH SOUTHGATE"
